About CTU-Buddy

Welcome to CTU-Buddy, an innovative educational system developed by a dedicated team at City Technical University.

Our mission is to enhance the learning experience for students by providing them with a user-friendly platform to manage their timetables, engage in discussions, and share educational resources.

With CTU-Buddy, students can collaborate with their peers, access valuable study materials, and stay organized throughout their academic journey.
